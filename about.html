Our Heritage

THE CHAIWALA STORY

ROOTED IN TRADITION

Inspired by the bustling streets of Hyderabad and the serene tea gardens of Assam, Chaiwala Shop was born out of a desire to bring authentic, high-quality chai to the modern world.

We believe that chai is not just a drink; it's a ritual. It's the pause in a busy day, the conversation starter, and the comfort of home. Every cup we brew is a tribute to the Deccan way of life.

CRAFTED FOR YOU

Our chefs use age-old recipes passed down through generations. From the slow-cooked Haleem to the crisp, buttery Osmania biscuits, we ensure every bite transports you back to the royal kitchens of the Nizams.
